Privacy Policy for CraftPachnad

Last Updated: March 2026

Introduction

CraftPachnad ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the CraftPachnad mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Phone Number: Used for authentication and account security.
  • Name: Used to personalize banners and app experience.
  • Profile Photo: Uploaded by you and stored securely.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and app version.
  • Usage Data: App interactions and feature usage.

User-Generated Content

  • Photos and images uploaded by users
  • Banner designs created inside the app

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve app features
  • To authenticate users securely
  • To store and manage user-created banners
  • To prevent misuse, fraud, or abuse
  • To provide customer support

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform)
  • Industry-standard security measures are applied
  • We do not sell or share personal data with advertisers

Third-Party Services

CraftPachnad uses trusted third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Storage
  • Google Sign-In

SMS Permissions

The app may request SMS permissions (RECEIVE_SMS / READ_SMS) only to automatically detect One-Time Passwords (OTP) for phone number verification.

  • SMS content is not read, stored, or shared
  • Permissions are used only for login verification
  • No SMS data is sent to any third party

Your Rights

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Delete your account at any time

Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as required to provide services. Users may request permanent deletion at any time.

Children’s Privacy

CraftPachnad is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
